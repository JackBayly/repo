Gazpacho

Home

Description

Gazpacho is a healthy vegetable and tomato soup served cold. It's a refreshing summer drink and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 4 tomatoes
  • Half a cucumber
  • Half a green or red bell pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Blend all ingredients together.
  2. Let it chill in the fridge for at least 3 hours.
  3. Serve cold.
