Dolma

Home

Description

Dolma is a vegetarian main or side dish local to various Mediterranean countries. Typically, it consists of cooked grape leaves stuffed with rice, olive oil, lemon juice, and various spices

Ingredients

  • 1 (16-ounce) jar grape leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 1 medium yellow onion, finely diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 1 tablespoon parsley
  • 1 tablespoon basil
  • 1 1/2 cups uncookedwhite rice, long-grain, such as basmati
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts, raw, optional
  • 8 cups vegetable broth, divided
  • 2/3 cups lemon juice, divided, plus more for drizzling

Directions

  1. Saute onions in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir in the rice and hot water. Cover it.
  3. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat. Mix in tomato paste, nuts, and spices. Let it cool.
  5. Put a large plate face-down in a large pot.
  6. Rinse grape leaves and cut off any stems.
  7. Put one teaspoon of rice mix in the center of a leaf. Roll it into a cigar shape and place in pot. Repeat with other ingredients.
  8. Pour in water to cover the layer of dolmas. Cover and simmer on low for 45 minutes. Check to add more water occasionally.
