Spanish Omelette

Home

Description

Tortilla or Spanish omellette in English, is a simple Spanish dish made of potatoes and eggs. It's a great appetizer or main dish for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.It can be eaten hot or cold.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 a cup of olive oil
  • 5 medium potatoes, cut in half-wedges
  • 1 medium onion, diced (optional)
  • 8 eggs
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Fry the potatoes and onions on a non-stick pan with the olive oil.
  2. Beat the eggs in a bowl and pour over potatoes on pan. Make sure everything is leveled in the pan.
  3. Place a big plate on the pan and flip the tortilla, then carefully slide it back into the pan to cook the other side.
  4. Cook until solid and light brown. Occasionally, gently shaking the pan.
  5. Remove by flipping it onto a clean plate.
  6. Serve warm or cold.
